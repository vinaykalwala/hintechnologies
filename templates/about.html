About HIN Technologies

Empowering IT Professionals with Real DevOps Skills Since 2018

Our Story

At HIN Technologies, we recognized a critical gap between academic IT education and industry requirements. Fresh graduates knew theory but struggled with real-world tools like Docker, Kubernetes, and Jenkins. Experienced professionals needed structured upskilling to stay relevant.

We built HIN Technologies to solve this problem. Our training approach focuses on hands-on learning with actual cloud environments, live projects, and industry-standard tools. Every student builds a complete CI/CD pipeline from scratch – not just watch demos.

Our Mission

"To bridge the DevOps skills gap in India by delivering practical, job-focused training that transforms beginners into confident professionals."

Our Training Approach

1️⃣ Learn by Doing

No PowerPoint-heavy sessions. Every concept is followed by a live hands-on lab exercise.

2️⃣ Real-World Projects

Work on projects that simulate actual enterprise scenarios – multiple environments, rollback strategies, monitoring setup.

3️⃣ Industry Expert Trainers

All trainers are practicing DevOps engineers with 6+ years of experience in product companies and consultancies.

4️⃣ Career-Focused Support

Resume reviews, mock interviews, GitHub portfolio building, and referral to our hiring partner network.

  • Linux, Git & Maven – Shell scripting, Git workflows, Maven setup
  • Code Quality & Artifact Management – SonarQube, JFrog Artifactory
  • Docker & Kubernetes – Containers, pods, deployments, scaling
  • Monitoring & Observability – Datadog, New Relic
  • Configuration Management & IaC – Ansible, Terraform
  • Project Management – Jira, Agile ticketing
  • Capstone Project: End-to-end CI/CD pipeline
